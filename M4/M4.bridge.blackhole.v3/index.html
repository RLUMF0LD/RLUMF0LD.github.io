Abstract:  
This module redefines the black hole as a phase inversion mirror within the framework of Collapse Geometry (CG).

(D → 0, A → A⁻¹, T → ∞) ⇒ Phase Mirror Condition

light (T → 0) is its dual exhalation phase.  
Thus, the black hole and light represent two complementary ends of a single tensorial breath.

In CG, the black hole is not the endpoint of physics but the mirror where physics observes its own syntax.  
The “singularity” is a linguistic saturation rather than an ontological void.
